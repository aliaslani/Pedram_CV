Microwave Engineer

Working on a tight schedule at HUAWEI, changed lots of things in my life. Shoulder to shoulder with other overwhelmed teammates in a dynamic career, I had to fulfill different roles in a team. Despite the initial period during which I worked as an "Implementor", I was then held responsible for Material Management Tasks, and soon I was promoted to the title of Material Manager of Microwave Team. These tight conditions, being worsened by such serious impediments as underdeveloped infrastructure and untrained subcontractors, influenced my perception of career life. My eyes were opened to the significance of "knowledge" as the only counteracting factor against the inexorable waste of time in a glamorous job like that! I participated in Claud Networking trainings held by the company and began to learn Python, hoping to address the issue of our disorganized inventories with a software-based solution.
